## üìä External Data Sources (Not Included)

This repository does **not** include any external datasets. It provides a pathway to integrate the following sources, assuming appropriate access:

- **SCImago Journal Rank (SJR)**  
  Source: SCImago Journal & Country Rank  
  Access: Publicly available at https://www.scimagojr.com  
  Data includes SJR score, H-index, and subject categories.

- **Journal Citation Reports (JCR)**  
  Source: Clarivate Analytics  
  Access: Via La Trobe University's institutional subscription  
  Data includes Journal Impact Factor (JIF) and 5-Year JIF.

- **CiteScore and SNIP**  
  Source: Elsevier CiteScore Metrics  
  Access: Via La Trobe University's institutional subscription  
  Data includes CiteScore, SNIP, and Open Access status.
